# Opportunity Overview

## Opportunity Details
- **Opportunity Name:** Community Services Digital Transformation - Salesforce Implementation
- **Issuing Organization:** Department of Community and Justice (DCJ), New South Wales Government
- **Industry/Sector:** Government - Human Services
- **Geographic Scope:** New South Wales, Australia (with national expansion potential)
- **Estimated Contract Value:** AUD $8.5M - $12M (implementation) + AUD $2.5M - $4M (optional support)
- **Contract Type:** Professional Services + Software Licensing + Support Services
- **Incumbent:** Legacy system managed by internal IT team (mainframe-based COBOL system from 1987)

## Scope Summary
DCJ requires a greenfield Salesforce implementation to replace their legacy case management system serving 15,000+ community services workers across NSW. The solution must handle sensitive personal information with absolute security while providing a mobile-first, user-friendly platform for child protection, disability services, housing assistance, and community support programs with real-time collaboration across 200+ offices.
