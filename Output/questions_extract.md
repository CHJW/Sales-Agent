## Key Constraints and Requirements

### Page Limits by Section:
- **Section A (Executive Summary):** 5 pages maximum
- **Section B (Technical Response):** 20 pages maximum  
- **Section C (Implementation Approach):** 15 pages maximum
- **Section D (Team & Experience):** 10 pages maximum
- **Section E (Pricing):** 5 pages maximum
- **Total:** 55 pages maximum for written response

### Critical Pass/Fail Requirements:
All vendors must meet mandatory requirements in Section 4.3 to proceed to evaluation:
1. Current Salesforce Premier Partner status
2. Minimum 5 government Salesforce implementations >$2M
3. Security clearance capability (minimum NV1 for key personnel) 
4. Local Australian delivery team (minimum 80%)
5. ISO 27001 certification
6. Professional Indemnity Insurance ($20M minimum)

### Special Notes:
- Case studies are specifically required for government sector experience (Q12)
- Integration approach must address the 5 priority systems detailed in Appendix A
- All pricing must be in AUD excluding GST with specific breakdown categories
- Information session attendance is mandatory for all bidders
- CVs required for all key personnel with Salesforce certifications listed
