## Opportunity Overview

The Department of Community and Justice (DCJ) in New South Wales is seeking to replace their 37-year-old COBOL mainframe system with a modern Salesforce implementation serving 15,000+ community services workers across 200+ offices. This greenfield implementation addresses critical child protection, disability services, housing assistance, and community support programs requiring absolute security for sensitive personal information with mobile-first, real-time collaboration capabilities.
