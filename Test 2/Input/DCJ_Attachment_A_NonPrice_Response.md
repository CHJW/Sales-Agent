## DOCUMENT CONTROL AND INSTRUCTIONS

### Document Purpose and Scope
This document contains the non-price evaluation criteria for the NSW Department of Communities and Justice Salesforce Application Managed Services procurement. The responses provided in this document will be evaluated against weighted criteria totaling 90% of the overall evaluation score (pricing represents 10%).

### Critical Response Requirements
You must complete all sections within the specified page limits and formatting requirements. Failure to comply with these requirements may result in reduced scoring or disqualification.

### Evaluation Context
Your responses will be evaluated by a technical panel including:
- **DCJ Senior Management:** Strategic alignment and business value assessment
- **DCJ Technical Team:** Technical capability and solution appropriateness
- **DCJ Procurement Specialists:** Commercial viability and compliance
- **External Subject Matter Experts:** Independent technical validation
- **Security Advisors:** Security and compliance assessment

### Response Quality Expectations
- **Evidence-Based:** All claims must be supported by specific examples and evidence
- **Quantifiable Outcomes:** Include measurable results and performance metrics
- **Relevant Examples:** Focus on experiences most relevant to DCJ's requirements
