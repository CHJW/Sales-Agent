## INSTRUCTIONS

This pricing schedule must be completed in full for your proposal to be considered compliant. All pricing must be in Australian Dollars (AUD) and exclusive of GST unless otherwise stated.

### Pricing Requirements:
- All prices are to be fixed for the initial 3-year term
- Annual CPI adjustments apply for extension periods (capped at 4% per annum)
- Provide both resource rates and fixed monthly service fees
- Include all costs - no hidden charges permitted
- Pricing must be valid for 180 days from submission deadline

### Pricing Model:
DCJ requires a hybrid pricing model combining:
1. **Fixed Monthly Managed Service Fees** - for base service delivery
2. **Resource-Based Rates** - for enhancement and project work
3. **Optional Services** - for additional capabilities as required

### 7.1 Payment Terms

**Standard Payment Terms:** Net 30 days from invoice date
**Early Payment Discount:** 2% if paid within 10 days
**Late Payment Interest:** 1.5% per month on overdue amounts

### 7.2 Price Adjustment Mechanism

**Initial Term (Years 1-3):** Fixed pricing - no adjustments
**Extension Periods:** Annual CPI adjustment, maximum 4% per annum
**Adjustment Date:** Anniversary of contract commencement
**CPI Reference:** Reserve Bank of Australia Consumer Price Index

### 7.3 Currency and GST

**Currency:** All prices in Australian Dollars (AUD)
**GST Treatment:** All prices exclusive of GST unless stated
**Foreign Exchange:** Not applicable - all services delivered in Australia

### 7.4 Benchmarking and Market Testing

**Benchmarking:** Agreed to market rate benchmarking every 24 months
**Market Testing:** Rates to remain competitive with market
**Adjustment Process:** Mutual agreement on any rate adjustments
