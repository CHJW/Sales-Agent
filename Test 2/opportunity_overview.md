## Scope Summary

DCJ is seeking comprehensive Application Managed Services for their Salesforce platform as part of a $485 million digital transformation program replacing 47 legacy systems with an integrated Salesforce ecosystem. The AMS engagement will provide ongoing support, maintenance, configuration, and enhancement services for Service Cloud, Experience Cloud, MuleSoft integration platform, and analytics capabilities serving 15,000+ staff across child protection, social housing, disability services, and youth justice programs. This represents one of the largest government human services technology modernization initiatives in Australia, aiming to create a unified, client-centric service delivery platform with 99.7% availability targets.
