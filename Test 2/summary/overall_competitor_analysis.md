## Competitive Landscape Overview

The NSW DCJ Salesforce AMS opportunity represents one of Australia's largest government human services technology contracts. The competitive landscape features established global consulting firms competing against specialized Salesforce system integrators for this strategic 7-year engagement.

### Key Market Dynamics
- **High-Stakes Government Contract:** Largest human services technology transformation in Australia
- **Complex Technical Requirements:** Salesforce Service Cloud, Experience Cloud, and MuleSoft integration
- **Specialized Domain Knowledge:** Child protection, social housing, disability services, and youth justice
- **Security and Compliance Focus:** Government security clearances and data sovereignty requirements
- **Social Procurement Emphasis:** 25% weighting on SME & Aboriginal participation

### Primary Competitive Threats

Based on analysis of the competitive landscape, two major competitors have been identified as primary threats:

1. **Deloitte Australia** - Big 4 consulting firm with strong government relationships
2. **Capgemini** - Global technology services with deep Salesforce partnership
