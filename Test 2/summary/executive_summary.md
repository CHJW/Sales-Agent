## Opportunity Overview

The NSW Department of Communities and Justice (DCJ) Salesforce Application Managed Services represents Australia's largest government human services digital transformation opportunity. This $69.7M-$101.3M seven-year engagement will provide comprehensive managed services for DCJ's Salesforce ecosystem, supporting 15,000+ staff across 334 offices serving 500,000+ clients in child protection, social housing, disability services, and youth justice.

**Key Opportunity Metrics:**
- **Annual Contract Value:** $8.5M - $16.0M over 7 years
- **Strategic Scope:** Application Managed Services for Salesforce Service Cloud, Experience Cloud, and MuleSoft integration platform
- **Geographic Coverage:** Metropolitan, regional, and remote NSW locations
- **Service Requirements:** 24/7 support with 99.7% availability target
- **Extension Options:** Two additional 2-year extensions beyond initial 3-year term

This engagement represents the cornerstone of DCJ's $485 million digital transformation program, replacing 47 legacy systems with an integrated Salesforce ecosystem. The successful vendor will become DCJ's strategic technology partner for Australia's most comprehensive human services modernization initiative.
